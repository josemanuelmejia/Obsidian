Political crises are junctures in which gold is bought as a heaven asset; but the reason why is because the distrust on a particular currency as the US Dollar, rise the demand for the money-commodity which is gold.

>  Political crises which destroy "confidence" in a particular currency, immediately generate a demand for the general value-equivalent (for commodity-money).

[innescapitalismandgold1981, página 28](innescapitalismandgold1981.pdf#page=28&selection=58,8,60,48)

In the current juncture, due to the military offensive initiated by Israel and US against Iran, the confidence on the US Dollar increased. Pushing its demand and consequently diminishing the attractiveness of gold. Gold price fall since the US-Israel war began.
