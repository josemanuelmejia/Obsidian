The concept of a Global Police State as presented in the document D 2494 encompasses several interrelated developments that reflect the emerging character of global society and economy as a repressive totality. Here are the key aspects:

1. Mass Social Control and Repression: The Global Police State is characterized by omnipresent systems of social control, repression, and warfare aimed at containing potential rebellions from the global working class and surplus humanity. This includes methods such as border walls, deportation regimes, mass incarceration, and advanced surveillance technologies[1](https://go.atlasti.com/38054c95-5292-4f13-88cd-5dfac9eddf05/documents/11ba487e-4832-4a62-911c-e1f16253bdcc/range/1125-2059) [2](https://go.atlasti.com/38054c95-5292-4f13-88cd-5dfac9eddf05/documents/11ba487e-4832-4a62-911c-e1f16253bdcc/range/530-1402). 

2. Militarized Accumulation: The global economy increasingly relies on the development and deployment of systems of warfare and social control as a means of profit-making. This phenomenon, termed "militarized accumulation," suggests that the ruling groups have a vested interest in war and repression as mechanisms for capital accumulation, especially in the face of economic stagnation.

3. Political Systems and Fascism: The rise of neo-fascist and authoritarian movements globally is seen as part of the Global Police State's emergence. This includes a polarization between leftist movements and far-right factions, with the latter often exhibiting openly fascist tendencies. The document argues that this political climate is conducive to the establishment of a totalitarian state[3](https://go.atlasti.com/38054c95-5292-4f13-88cd-5dfac9eddf05/documents/11ba487e-4832-4a62-911c-e1f16253bdcc/range/1186-1984) [4](https://go.atlasti.com/38054c95-5292-4f13-88cd-5dfac9eddf05/documents/11ba487e-4832-4a62-911c-e1f16253bdcc/range/848-2871). 

4. Economic and Political Intersections: The Global Police State is not merely a political phenomenon but is deeply intertwined with economic structures. The document posits that the dynamics of global capitalism, particularly its crises and transformations, are crucial to understanding the rise of the Global Police State. It emphasizes that movements against this state must recognize global capitalism as the root cause of the systems of control and repression they oppose.

5. Technological Integration: The document highlights how advancements in technology, particularly digitalization, are utilized to enhance state control and repression. This includes the use of AI, surveillance systems, and militarized policing, which blur the lines between civilian and military operations[9](https://go.atlasti.com/38054c95-5292-4f13-88cd-5dfac9eddf05/documents/11ba487e-4832-4a62-911c-e1f16253bdcc/range/517-1359) [10](https://go.atlasti.com/38054c95-5292-4f13-88cd-5dfac9eddf05/documents/11ba487e-4832-4a62-911c-e1f16253bdcc/range/2236-2786). 

In summary, the Global Police State is depicted as a complex interplay of social control, economic interests, and political authoritarianism, all exacerbated by technological advancements and the ongoing crises of global capitalism.
