Text scheme

1. **Introduction**: Context of capital development and its relationship to human reproduction.
2. **Capital Fixed and Circulante**: Definitions and key differences.
3. **Domination of Labour by Capital**: Impact of technique and science on the capital-to-work relationship.
4. **Plusvalry and Accumulation**: Capture of surplus work as an engine of capital.
5. **Contractions of Fijo Capital**: Dilems derived from the technological dependence.
6. **Distopical Capital Future**: Reflection on a machine-dominated system.
7. **Conclusion**: Balance on the transformations and contradictions of capitalism.
