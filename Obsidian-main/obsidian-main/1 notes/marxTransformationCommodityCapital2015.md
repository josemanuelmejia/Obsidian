At the Money-Commodity-Money' circuit of capital, Marx separates this process in two moments or phases. The first one distinguish different types of capital.
The Commodity-dealing capital is the second moment in the circuit of reproduction. This is called "the movement of commodity capital".
Commodity capital: Capital in it's commodity form.
Commodity capital: materials for capitalist production or circulation.

Commodity-dealing capital: the circulation of capital by the circuit of reproduction MCM' acquires "independent life" as it repeats with a particular capital(ist) and with a particular division of labor, *is a function of the political dominance of those capitalist which enforce the control over the more profitable branches of industry and the territories and materials which that capitalist production in special operates, this is a feature of imperialism.*
