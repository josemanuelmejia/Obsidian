137 - Funding History: An Inside Look at Foundation and Government Decisionmaking

Friday, January 5, 2024

3:30 PM - 5:00 PM

-   Continental Ballroom 6 (Ballroom, Hilton Union Square)

##### Description

This roundtable discussion explores how foundations consider and make decisions about funding in higher education. Panelists from several leading foundations will share how their organizations establish funding priorities and determine how best to support the work of historians.
