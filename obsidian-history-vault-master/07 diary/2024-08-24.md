Octubre: Revista marxista de ciencias sociales y economía política

October: Journal of Marxist Social Sciences and Political Economy

The development of critical theory at the present historical juncture is an instrument of struggle. Political sociology as a social science understanding the built of power from the bottom to the top, was excerpted from political economy. The critical power of historical political sociology was constrained by its divorce with the multiverse of Marxism, with differentiated ratios between the mainstream and the radical approaches for social change that are an integral part of it.

Nevertheless, as the events presented, the most critical theories of globalization, world-system research, contentious politics and social movement studies, as the mainstream branches of historical political sociology lacked, denied or rejected an exposure to a Marxist critique or theoretical bridge for advanced sophistication. But if this critique would be imprisoned on the headquarters of some intelligentsia, the dead knowledge of the most studious and snootiest of all scholars, the philologists and schoolmen who are both these things only by profession (Nietzsche :102 for the servitude of the power of capital will born again as painted in the final stages of the Orozco’s Mural finished in 1931

My research on antisystemic movements seeks to contribute to fill the theoretical gap between Marxism and historical political sociology, by recognizing the class struggle of the Andean indigenous cholo peasantry against the finance capital reproduction of the top international bank state capitalists’ trusts operating by the circuit of mining accumulation. And developing theory instrumental for their struggle against dispossession and destruction of land and water, as the vital values for social reproduction not only of the peasantry but for the proletariat of the cities surrounding the exploited mega-mines.

La sociología política que entiende la construcción del poder desde abajo hacia arriba, fue extraída de la economía política. El poder crítico de la sociología política histórica se vio limitado por su divorcio con el multiverso del marxismo, con proporciones diferenciadas entre la corriente principal y los enfoques radicales para el cambio social que son una parte integral de él.
